Will this get me to B2?
Honestly: the grammar progression and ~830 core words here are a real, solid path through B1 and into B2 grammar and reading. But B2 typically assumes 4,000–6,000 words and real speaking fluency — no self-study app gets you there alone. To actually land and hold B2, pair this with:
1. A weekly conversation partner or tutor (italki, Tandem) — speaking output can't be self-taught.
2. One real Dutch news article a week from week 50 onward (NOS, NRC) — beyond this app's core vocab.
3. The official Staatsexamen NT2 practice exams (weeks 65–67, 101–102) under real time pressure.
Do all three alongside this roadmap and B2 is a realistic, achievable target.
